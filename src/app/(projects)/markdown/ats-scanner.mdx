Scan resumes. Uncover the truth. Judge for the right candidate.

Inspired by Papers, Please, but with a twist: instead of stamping papers, you wield a laser scanner to reveal the hidden contents of each procedurally generated CV. Some candidates might seem perfect... until you dig deeper.

## What to expect:
- Randomly generated resumes with subtle (or not-so-subtle) red flags.
- A laser scanning mechanic that lets you uncover hidden text in real time.
- Start as an intern, and progress through tougher hiring levels as the resumes (and lies) get harder to read.
- Each playthrough is different, and hilariously suspicious.

## Controls:
- Simple controls: Just move your mouse and click (LMB). No keyboard needed!
