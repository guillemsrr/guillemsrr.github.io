Argument:

You play the role of Marwan's father, a young boy who had to live war and immigration through the ocean, as so many refugees nowadays.

This is a text-based game, while you drive the boat to Europe.



Languages:

English, spanish and catalan.



Controls:

WAD -> Boat Movement.

Arrows /Mouse wheel / Mouse hover -> change text.

Space / Enter / Middle mouse button / Mouse click -> select text.
